Tomato Egg Curry

Description

Welcome to our culinary haven! Here, we present to you an irresistible recipe that will tantalize your taste buds - Tomato Egg Curry. Bursting with rich flavors and loaded with essential nutrients, this hearty dish is a perfect blend of tangy tomatoes and velvety eggs. Simple to prepare and enjoyable for all, this curry will undoubtedly become a favorite in your kitchen. Let's dive into the world of flavors and create a memorable dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • Small ball of tamarind (approximately the size of a lime)
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to spice preference)
    • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste and water as needed

Steps

  1. Boil 4 large eggs until cooked (8-10 minutes), then peel and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and sauté 1 teaspoon cumin seeds until they splutter.
  3. Add 1 finely chopped large onion, 3 minced garlic cloves, and grated ginger. Sauté until onions are golden.
  4. Mix in 4 finely chopped ripe tomatoes and 2-3 chopped green chilies. Cook until tomatoes soften and release juices.
  5. Add 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder, and salt to taste. Stir well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Gently place boiled eggs in the tomato mixture, partially submerging them. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes to infuse flavors.
  7. Sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon garam masala and garnish with 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with rice, naan, or paratha. Enjoy your delicious Tomato Egg Curry!
